Key Features to Consider

When shopping for wireless earbuds under $100, there are several key features you should consider to ensure you make a wise purchase:

  • Sound Quality: Look for earbuds with good bass response and clarity.
  • Battery Life: Aim for at least 4-6 hours of playback time on a single charge, with additional charges provided by the case.
  • Comfort and Fit: Choose earbuds that come with multiple ear tips for a snug fit, especially if you plan to wear them for extended periods.
  • Water Resistance: If you plan on using them for workouts, look for a rating of at least IPX4.
  • Connectivity: Ensure they support Bluetooth 5.0 or higher for better range and stability.
