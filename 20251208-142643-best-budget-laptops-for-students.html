What to Look for in a Budget Laptop

Before diving into our top recommendations, it's important to understand what to consider when choosing a budget laptop:

  • Performance: Look for laptops with at least an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processor. These will handle basic tasks like web browsing, document editing, and streaming.
  • RAM: A minimum of 8GB of RAM is recommended for smooth multitasking.
  • Storage: An SSD (Solid State Drive) is preferred for faster boot times and application loading. Aim for at least 256GB.
  • Battery Life: A laptop that lasts at least 8 hours on a single charge is ideal for long days on campus.
  • Display: A Full HD (1920 x 1080) display is preferable for clarity, especially if you'll be working on visuals or watching videos.
