Key Features to Look for in a Budget Laptop

When searching for the best budget laptop, consider the following features:

  • Performance: Look for a decent processor (Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3), adequate RAM (at least 8GB), and sufficient storage (SSD preferred for speed).
  • Battery Life: A long-lasting battery is essential for students who are always on the go.
  • Display Quality: A clear and bright screen will make it easier to read and work on assignments.
  • Portability: Lightweight and compact designs are preferable for easy transport between classes.
  • Build Quality: A durable build will ensure your laptop withstands daily wear and tear.
