Key Features to Look For

When shopping for a portable charger, consider the following features to ensure you choose the right one for your travel needs:

  • Battery Capacity: Measured in milliamp hours (mAh), a higher capacity means more charges for your devices.
  • Size and Weight: Opt for something lightweight and compact that easily fits in your bag or pocket.
  • Charging Speed: Look for fast charging options like Quick Charge or Power Delivery.
  • Number of Ports: More ports allow you to charge multiple devices simultaneously.
  • Durability: A robust build can withstand the rigors of travel.
