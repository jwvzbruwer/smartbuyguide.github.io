What to Look for in a Budget Laptop

Before diving into our recommendations, it‚Äôs crucial to understand what features are most important in a budget laptop for students. Here are some key factors to consider:

1. Performance

Look for laptops with at least an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processor. These provide sufficient power for everyday tasks like web browsing, document editing, and video streaming.

2. RAM

A minimum of 8GB of RAM is recommended for smooth multitasking. This ensures that you can run multiple applications without lag, especially during online classes or while working on assignments.

3. Battery Life

Students are often on the go, so a laptop with a long battery life is essential. Aim for a device that can last at least 8 hours on a single charge.

4. Display

A full HD display (1920 x 1080 resolution) provides a better viewing experience, making it easier to read text and watch videos.

5. Portability

Consider the weight and size of the laptop. A lightweight laptop is easier to carry around campus and fits better in a backpack.
