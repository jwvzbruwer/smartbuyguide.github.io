What to Look for in a Fitness Tracker

Before diving into our top recommendations, it‚Äôs important to understand what features to consider when selecting a fitness tracker:

  • Activity Tracking: Look for trackers that monitor steps, distance, calories burned, and active minutes.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring: Continuous heart rate tracking can provide deeper insights into your health.
  • GPS Functionality: Built-in GPS is essential for runners and cyclists who want accurate distance tracking.
  • Battery Life: Longer battery life means less frequent charging and more time spent tracking.

  • Additional Features: Consider features like sleep tracking, water resistance, and smart notifications.
