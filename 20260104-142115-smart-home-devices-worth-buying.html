What Are Smart Home Devices?

Smart home devices are gadgets that connect to your home network and can be controlled remotely via smartphones, tablets, or voice commands. They utilize the Internet of Things (IoT) technology, allowing users to automate and enhance various functions around the home. Whether you're looking to improve security, save on energy bills, or simply add convenience to your daily routine, smart home devices provide a plethora of options to suit your needs.
