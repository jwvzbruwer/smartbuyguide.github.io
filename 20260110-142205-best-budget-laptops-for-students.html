What to Look for in a Budget Laptop

Before diving into our recommendations, it's essential to know what features are most important for students. Here are the key factors to consider:

Performance

For general student tasks like word processing, web browsing, and streaming, a laptop with at least an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processor is recommended. Look for at least 8GB of RAM to ensure smooth multitasking.

Battery Life

Long battery life is crucial for students who spend long hours on campus. Aim for a laptop that offers at least 8 hours of usage on a single charge.

Portability

A lightweight and slim design makes it easy to carry your laptop around campus. Look for devices weighing around 3-4 pounds and with a screen size of 13 to 15 inches.

Storage

Consider SSDs over HDDs for faster boot times and quicker access to your files. A minimum of 256GB SSD is ideal for storing documents, presentations, and media.
